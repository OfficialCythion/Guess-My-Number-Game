How To Play:



The aim of the game is simple. You must guess the correct number between 1 & 20. The "?" represents the secret number which will be revealed once you guess it correctly. The secret number is random and changes each time you play. Click inside the "search" bar to enter your number. To check your answer, click the "check" button. A prompt will show on screen if you're guessing too high or too low, allowing you to adjust your guesses accordingly. Each time you guess incorrectly, your score will decrease. Reach a score of "0" and you will lose the game. A highscore will be set allowing you to try and beat your previous score. The "restart" button will start the game again with a new secret number.
